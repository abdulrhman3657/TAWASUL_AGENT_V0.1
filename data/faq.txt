1. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and select “Buy Now, Pay Later” options such as Klarna and Afterpay.

2. Is my payment information secure?

Yes. All transactions are processed through encrypted payment gateways that comply with PCI DSS security standards. We never store your card details.

3. Do I need to create an account to place an order?

No, you can check out as a guest. However, creating an account allows you to track orders, manage returns, and receive special offers.

4. How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you’ll receive a tracking link via email or SMS. You can also log in to your account to track the delivery status anytime.

5. How long will it take to receive my order?

Standard shipping usually takes 3–7 business days, depending on your location. Express delivery options are available at checkout for faster service.

6. Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping times and fees vary based on destination and local customs regulations.

7. How much does shipping cost?

Shipping is free for orders over $50. For smaller orders, shipping fees are calculated at checkout based on your address and selected shipping method.

8. Can I change or cancel my order after placing it?

Orders can be changed or canceled within one hour of placement. After that, the order is likely already in processing and can’t be modified.

9. What should I do if I entered the wrong address?

Contact our customer support as soon as possible at support@company.com
. If the order hasn’t shipped yet, we can update the address.

10. What if my order hasn’t arrived yet?

Check your tracking information first. If it shows “delivered” but you haven’t received it, contact our support team for assistance.

11. What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for most items, provided they are unused and in original packaging. Some exclusions apply (e.g., final sale items).

12. How do I start a return?

Go to your order history, click “Request Return,” select the item, and print the prepaid return label. Drop the package at your nearest shipping point.

13. When will I receive my refund?

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, your refund will be processed within 3–5 business days. Refunds appear in your account within 7–10 days.

14. Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?

Yes. If the replacement is in stock, we’ll process an exchange once the original item is returned. Exchanges are subject to availability.

15. What should I do if I received a damaged or defective product?

Please contact support@company.com
 within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the issue. We’ll send a replacement or issue a full refund.

16. Do you offer gift wrapping or personalized messages?

Yes, gift wrapping and message cards are available at checkout for a small additional fee.

17. Can I use multiple promo codes on one order?

Only one promo code can be applied per order. However, promo codes can be combined with automatic discounts such as free shipping offers.

18. How can I use a gift card?

Enter the gift card code at checkout. The balance will automatically apply to your total. If your order exceeds the balance, you can pay the difference.

19. How do I know what size to order?

Each product page includes a detailed size chart. You can also use our “Find My Fit” tool for personalized recommendations.

20. Are your products authentic?

Yes. All products sold on our website are 100% authentic and sourced directly from verified suppliers or official brand partners.

21. Do you restock sold-out items?

Popular items are often restocked. You can click “Notify Me” on a product page to receive an email when the item becomes available again.

22. How do I contact customer support?

You can reach us by email at support@company.com
 or through live chat on our website, available Monday–Friday, 9 AM–6 PM (local time).

23. Do you offer wholesale or bulk discounts?

Yes. For orders over 50 units, contact sales@company.com
 for wholesale pricing and partnership opportunities.

24. What should I do if I forgot my password?

Go to the login page and click “Forgot Password.” You’ll receive an email with a secure link to reset your password.

25. Can I subscribe to your newsletter?

Absolutely. Sign up via the footer on our website to receive updates on new arrivals, exclusive discounts, and seasonal sales.

26. Are there any hidden fees at checkout?

No. The total price displayed at checkout includes all applicable taxes and fees. International orders may incur customs duties based on your country.

27. How can I apply for a return if I bought as a guest?

Visit the “Track My Order” page, enter your email and order number, then click “Return an Item.”

28. Do you offer loyalty rewards?

Yes, our rewards program lets you earn points for purchases, reviews, and referrals. Points can be redeemed for discounts on future orders.

29. Can I preorder upcoming items?

Yes. Preorder products are clearly marked and shipped on the release date shown on the product page.

30. How do I delete my account or personal data?

Contact privacy@company.com to request account deletion or data export under GDPR/CCPA compliance.
